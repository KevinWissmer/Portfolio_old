This animation or model shows the motion of particles. In detail it estimates the temperature with the average kinetic energy of the particles. The Chart above shows the statistics, which should, regardles the initial situation, form the Maxwell-Boltzmann-distribution. In this animation it is very unlikely, that the chart will take the exact form of the distribution, but if we would increase the number of discs, this would form this after a little relaxation time. I have done this animation with those predicted results with C and Python, but the process costs a lot of performance. Therfore you can only find a glimpse of what is possible.
